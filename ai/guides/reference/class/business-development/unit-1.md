## Story Time!

### The Kiddie Pool Story

- Visual narrative using images: a kiddie pool (children's wading pool) is shown alone, then paired with an image of autumn leaves (kiddie pool + fall leaves)
- The story illustrates how combining familiar elements in an unexpected context can challenge our assumptions and mental models
- The point is to demonstrate how context shapes interpretation and how quickly we form conclusions based on incomplete pictures

### What's The Point?

- Bad outcomes often come from reasonable decisions
- Bad outcomes often come from reasonable decisions ***made with incomplete mental models***

## AI Careers Reframed

Common framing:

- AI Creates Opportunity
- Or.... AI Creates Disruption

Reframed:

- AI Creates Opportunity
- **AND** AI Creates Disruption

*The key insight is that it is not "this or that" -- it is both simultaneously. AI creates opportunity AND disruption at the same time. Framing it as an either/or choice is itself an incomplete mental model.*
